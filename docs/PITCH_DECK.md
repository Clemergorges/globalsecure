## Slide 6: Business Model
**How We Make Money:**
1.  **Interchange Fees:** We earn ~1% on every card transaction.
2.  **FX Spread:** 0.5-1% on Crypto<->Fiat conversions (vs 3-5% banks).
3.  **Transfer Fees:** Small flat fee for external withdrawals.
4.  **Premium Subscriptions:** (Future) Metal cards, higher limits.
